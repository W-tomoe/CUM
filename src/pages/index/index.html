School story
UCM believes that the Bible is a treasure of our Heavenly Father’s teaching, which can be creatively integrated with early childhood education to nurture the holistic development of young learners. Before children can understand right or wrong, if they are raised up through the power of God's word, they can return to the source of truth whenever they face difficulties or challenges. They will not to be swayed but find their own value in God's sovereignty and grace. Hence, UCM learners will become moral, intelligent and spiritually mature.
